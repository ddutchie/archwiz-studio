# ArchWiz Studio BETA v0.0.1
**Introducing ArchWiz Studio. Your node-based architectural playground.**

This initial release establishes the core of our hybrid-local visualization engine, optimized exclusively for the Mac.

### ✨ What's Inside
*   **The Variation Canvas**: A node-based environment (powered by React Flow) for infinite design branching and visual history.
*   **Local 4x AI Upscale**: Native CoreML enhancement using **UltraSharp** and **RealESRGAN**—process high-res renders privately on your Neural Engine.
*   **Nano Banana Generation**: Professional-grade architectural image generation powered by Google's latest AI models.
*   **7 Reference Modes**: Precision control using **Sketch**, **Site/Context**, **3D View**, and **Material** references.
*   **14+ Architectural Styles**: Instant styles from **Minimalist** and **Japandi** to **Brutalist** and **Post-Digital**.

### 🔒 Built for Privacy
*   **Hybrid Architecture**: Generation happens in the cloud for speed, but all upscaling and final storage stay local on your Mac.
*   **Secure Credit Sync**: Seamless account management and credit balances via Supabase.
